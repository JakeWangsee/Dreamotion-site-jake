About Dreamotion

Dreamotion is a professional indoor playground manufacturer based in Guangzhou, China. With over 8 years of experience, we specialize in designing, producing, and delivering customized soft play equipment to clients all over the world, including Vietnam, Singapore, UAE, Africa and more.

Our Mission

We aim to bring joyful and safe indoor play environments to children everywhere by offering cost-effective and high-quality one-stop solutions to our customers.

What Makes Us Different

  • ✅ In-house design and production with factory-direct pricing
  • ✅ Global delivery and multilingual support
  • ✅ Custom themes, layouts, and materials for every need
  • ✅ Experience in malls, schools, hotels, and private businesses
