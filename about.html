About Dreamotion

Dreamotion is a professional indoor playground manufacturer based in Guangzhou, China. With 8+ years of industry experience and a dedicated team of designers and engineers, we offer end-to-end solutions for kids play areas around the world.

Our mission is to create safe, creative, and inspiring environments that stimulate children's imagination and promote physical development. From shopping malls and schools to hotels and entertainment centers, our customized playgrounds fit all spaces and budgets.

We proudly serve clients across Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore, Africa, and many more regions.
