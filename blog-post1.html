5 Tips for Choosing Playground Equipment

Choosing the right equipment is key to creating a fun and safe indoor play experience. Here are 5 things you need to know:

  1. Know Your Space: Measure your area and ceiling height.
  2. Set a Budget: Total cost includes equipment, delivery, and installation.
  3. Pick a Theme: A good visual identity can boost attraction.
  4. Safety First: Check certifications, soft edges, anti-slip flooring.
  5. Work With a Factory: Cut out middlemen and get tailored design.
